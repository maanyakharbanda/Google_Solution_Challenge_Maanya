VOCAL VERSE

Vocal Verse is a type of assistive technology that reads digital text aloud. It can take words on a computer or other digital device and convert them into audio. On the other hand, it uses speech recognition technology to turn spoken words into written text. This technology involves complex algorithms that analyze the audio and match it to words and phrases in the language the software is designed to understand.
