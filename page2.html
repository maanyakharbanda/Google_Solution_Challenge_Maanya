Quality Education: Text-to-speech technology can help students with visual impairments to access educational materials. Speech-to-text technology can help students with hearing impairments to participate in classroom discussions and lectures.

Good Health and Well-being: Text-to-speech technology can help people with visual impairments to access health information and services. Speech-to-text technology can help people with hearing impairments to communicate with healthcare providers.Text-to-speech and speech-to-text technologies can help people with disabilities to access information

Peace,Justice and Strong Instituitions: These technologies can help people with hearing or speech impairments to communicate effectively with others, thereby promoting inclusivity. They can also help in the creation of audio books, which can be used to spread awareness about human rights and justice.
